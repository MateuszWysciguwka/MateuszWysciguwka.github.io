CupaGoovno

CupaGoovno is a mod for the game Cuphead - an indie 2D action game focusing on difficult boss fights. The mod provides a major overhaul of the game, modifying bosses' behaviors, giving them new attacks and rebalancing items. It also introduces new content: custom weapons, items, achievements and even first-ever custom boss. Furthermore, the boss is enhanced with machine learning algorithms, allowing him to tailor attacks to the player based on their performance. The algorithms were the topic of my bachelor thesis at Warsaw University of Technology.

YouTube and TikTok videos covering my mod reached millions of views in total. The development process taught me how to work with public deadlines and effectively incorporate user feedback.
